Research/Talks - How I Hacked My Way to the Security Sector

Title: How I Hacked My Way to the Security Sector {And why you should always do what you love, no matter what people say!}

Abstract:

It all started 30 years ago… hm, that’s a bit too much, let me try again.

It all started 18 years ago, when I found a book of C in my uncles’ library. How stupid can you be to read C at 12 years old you might ask? A lot, but this is what brought me here.

A story of pain, passion, blood, sweat and tears, which aims to help people stop carrying about what other people say, follow their passion, and do everything in their hands to achieve their goals.

From a young age script kiddie, failing to enter the faculty that most believed will give me a career and money to feed my family, to hacking 100s of vehicles, becoming the CTO of a cybersecurity company, and leading my own research team, I will get you through stories that most will relate, and some will cry upon.

Buckle up, it’s gonna be a bumpy one!

Presented in TROOPERS24 Storytellers track, on June 26th, 2024, in Heidelberg, Germany.
